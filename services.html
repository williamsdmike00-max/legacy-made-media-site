Common questions

Things owners ask before they book.

How long does the build take?
14 days from approval to live. Day 1 is a kickoff call. Day 7 you see a preview. Day 14 we go live and switch the systems on. After that it runs.
Do I own the website and the systems?
Yes. The website lives on your domain. The CRM is your account. The AI voice is configured for your number. If you ever leave us, you keep everything.
What if I already have a website?
If it's converting, we keep it and just install the lead capture, follow-up, and AI layer. If it's not, we replace it. Strategy call decides.
Can I cancel the monthly retainer?
Yes — month-to-month after the first 90 days. We're a partner, not a hostage situation. Most clients stay because the system pays for itself within the first 60 days.
Why don't you list the AI voice in the Starter?
Because we want it to actually work for you. Voice agents need a tuned funnel underneath them. We install the foundation first, then upgrade you to voice once leads are flowing — usually month 2 or 3.
Do you work with businesses outside my industry?
We focus on owner-operated trade businesses — HVAC, plumbing, construction, landscaping, cleaning, auto detail, salons. If you have phone-heavy lead flow and a service to sell, we can install for you.
