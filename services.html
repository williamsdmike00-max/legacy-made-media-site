Common questions

Things owners ask before they book.

How long does the build take?
Basic: under 7 days. Deluxe: 10–14 days. Premium + AI: 14 days. We work fast because we're tight on scope, not because we cut corners.
Is there a monthly fee?
No required monthly fee on any package — they're flat-fee, one-time. If you want ongoing edits, content updates, or performance monitoring, we offer Monthly Updates / Support as an add-on (custom quote based on your needs).
Do I own the website?
Yes. The website lives on your domain, the AI runs in your account, and the branding files are yours. If you ever leave us, you keep everything.
What's "Founders Pricing — First 10 Clients Only"?
Exactly what it says. We're booking the first 10 clients at this pricing to build case studies and reviews. Once we hit 10, prices increase. If you book now, your price is locked in for life — including future tweaks and add-ons.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes. Start with Basic or Deluxe and upgrade to Premium + AI whenever you're ready — we credit your original build toward the upgrade so you only pay the difference.
Do you work with businesses outside Dallas–Fort Worth?
Yes. We're DFW-based but we work with small businesses anywhere in the US. Everything happens over Zoom, email, and text — no in-person required.
