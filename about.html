About

We build the systems
local owners can't.

Trade businesses run on grit. They lose money on missed calls, dead follow-ups, and websites that look like 2014. We close that gap — with AI, automation, and the kind of build quality you'd expect from a brand twice your size.

Why we exist

A simple observation.

Most local-business owners are great at the work. They're not great at the systems around the work — capturing the lead, following up, looking professional online, never missing a call.

For decades, those systems were too expensive or too complex for a 1-truck or 5-truck operation. So owners settled. They missed leads. They watched competitors with worse craft win because they had better systems.

That changed. AI and automation collapsed the cost. A solo HVAC owner can now have the same lead-handling stack as a national franchise — for less than a part-time employee costs in a week.

Legacy Made Media is the company that installs that stack. Done-for-you, end-to-end, built around the way local owners actually run their day.

How we operate

Five principles. No exceptions.

1

Outcomes, not technology

We don't sell "AI agents." We sell "a system that answers your phone 24/7 so you never miss a job." Same thing — but only one of those is something an HVAC owner cares about.

2

Speed beats polish, until it doesn't

14 days to live. Then we optimize relentlessly. We'd rather ship something that captures leads on day 15 than something perfect on day 90.

3

Own your stack

Your website, your domain, your CRM, your numbers. If you ever leave us, you keep all of it. We're a partner, not a vendor lock-in.

4

Diagnose before we quote

Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute call where we find your real bottleneck. Sometimes it's not what you think. We tell you the truth — even if it costs us the deal.

5

If it works, we keep doing it. If it doesn't, we change it — fast.

Monthly performance reviews. Bi-weekly optimization calls on the Full System tier. The system you launch with is the worst version you'll ever have. Every month it gets sharper.
