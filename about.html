Why we exist

A simple observation.

Most local-business owners are great at the work. They're not great at the systems around the work — capturing leads, looking professional online, never missing a customer.

For decades, those systems were too expensive or too complex for a 1-truck or 5-truck operation. So owners settled for outdated websites, ignored Google profiles, and lost calls because they were on a job.

That changed. AI and automation collapsed the cost. A solo HVAC owner, a salon, an auto detailer, a roofer — anyone — can now have the same digital stack as a national brand for less than a part-time hire costs in a week.

Legacy Made Media installs that stack. Flat-fee. Veteran-owned. Built around how small business owners actually run their day.

How we operate

Five principles. No exceptions.

1

Outcomes, not technology

We don't sell "AI agents." We sell "a system that answers your phone 24/7 so you never miss a customer." Same thing — but only one of those is something an owner cares about.

2

Speed beats polish, until it doesn't

Basic in 7 days. Premium + AI in 14. Then we optimize relentlessly. We'd rather ship something that captures leads on day 8 than something perfect on day 90.

3

Own your stack

Your website, your domain, your CRM, your AI configuration. If you ever leave us, you keep all of it. We're a partner, not a vendor lock-in.

4

Honest pricing, honest scope

Flat-fee. No "you also need this $500 thing" surprises after you sign. What you see on the services page is what you pay.

5

If it works, we keep doing it. If it doesn't, we change it — fast.

The site you launch with is the worst version you'll ever have. Add the Monthly Updates / Support add-on and we sharpen it month over month.
